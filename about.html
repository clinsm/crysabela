ABOUT US

CRYSABELA

MANAGEMENT EFFICIENCY

CRYSABELA is headed by the CEO and supported by a highly efficient management team that is comprised of a highly competent, reliable, professional and efficient group of leaders. 

The Management “Team” compliment all aspects of the service provided to the client, as effective liaison is simplified by adequate attention being administered to specific requirements and any problem that may arise. 

GOALS

Cleaning services

  • To place great emphasis on customer service, which we will achieve through centralised administration services and decentralised operations in order to give operational staff the opportunity to concentrate all their time as productively as possible servicing our clients.
  • Creating a clean environment which is safe and healthy to stay in, work, and be

Transport & Logistics

  • To be acknowledged by our customers as an integral part of their supply chain and transportation requirements.
  • To provide efficient, logical and unrivalled logistics solutions to clients.
  • To offer a fully integrated service from a fully committed and equipped team, capable of meeting every related need

MISSION

  • Achieve a level of sustainable commercial success, measured growth and ongoing competitiveness in the cleaning and logistics industries by recognising the value of clients and being committed to meeting and exceeding their expectations.
  • To be recognised as the medium sized cleaning company of choice to commercial, residential, social, industrial and private market sectors within South Africa. We believe that is possible through the efficient delivery of a personalised and effective cleaning service.
  • To be the most reliable and efficient logistics service provider offering a broad spectrum of logistics solutions, committed to service excellence and renowned for our integrity and ability to deliver.
  • Attract, challenge & retain the most capable people in both industries.
  • Respect the communities and environment in which we operate.
  • Create a brand name for our products and services in the cleaning industry.
  • Creation of job opportunities for the youth.
  • Strive to identify and satisfy all our clients’ needs.
  • To supply cost effective and professional services.
  • Actively promote creation of job opportunities in every community we operate in.
  • Strive to ensure that each individual is enabled to reach their full potential through training, personal development and internal promotion policies.

Values

  • We are committed to the highest standards of ethics and integrity; thus considering the balance of power between political, social and economic interests 
  • We are responsible for the commercial world, residential world and general public at large. Our employees are the most valued assets of the company and are essential participants with a shared responsibility in fulfilling our mission. 
  • We recognise that the quality and performance of our employees are the key factors in achieving our success. Accordingly, our Human Resources policies and practices at CRYSABELA CLEANING & LOGISTICS SERVICES are built on the following:

Dedication 

To assisting every employee reach their full potential in both performance and reward.

Commitment 

To diversity, equal opportunity and fair treatment

Promotion

Promotion based on merit and from within wherever possible

cooperation

An organisation structure and future to promote employee involvement, open communication, teamwork & cooperation.

our divisions

CRYSABELA CLEANING SERVICES

This division of the company was founded under the motivation from the situation concerning the then very strict rules on cleanliness of business premises in the attempt to slow down the spread of the coronavirus. We believe that clean and safe environments are great for business and private dwelling.

We at CRYSABELA aim to make a huge impact in the cleaning industry. We have a unique, excellent and effective solution in making residential and commercial facilities clean and secure. We are certain that we will bring back a cleaner & safer environment

CRYSABELA TRANSPORT SERVICES & PROJECTS 

This division of the company is committed to providing an all-encompassing general freight and courier transport to and from our listed destinations in the required timeframe.
